## Unrestricted Access to Sensitive Business Flows

Unrestricted access to sensitive business flows means that an API exposes a sensitive business flow, without properly restricting access to it. This can lead to attackers exploiting these vulnerabilities to harm the business in various ways.

Here are the problems and consequences of unrestricted access to sensitive business flows:

* **Financial losses:** Attackers can exploit sensitive business flows to steal money, such as buying all the stock of a high-demand item at once and reselling for a higher price.
* **Disrupted operations:** Attackers can disrupt business operations by spamming the system, reserving all available time slots, or exploiting referral programs.
* **Reputational damage:** Security breaches can damage the business reputation.

Prevention strategies:

* **Identify sensitive business flows:** Businesses should identify business flows that could be harmful if accessed excessively.
* **Implement protection mechanisms:**
    * **Device fingerprinting:** Deny service to unexpected client devices.
    * **Human detection:** Use captcha or biometric solutions.
    * **Analyze user flow:** Detect non-human patterns, like abnormally fast user actions.
    * **Block suspicious IPs:** Block IP addresses of Tor exit nodes and well-known proxies.
    * **Secure machine-consumed APIs:** Secure APIs consumed directly by machines, such as developer and B2B APIs.
* **Insufficient logging and monitoring:** This allows attackers to gain unrestricted access because they can abuse systems without being noticed. Implement sufficient logging to track suspicious activity and ensure log integrity. Continuously monitor API infrastructure.

Remember, these are just some of the problems, consequences and prevention strategies. It is important to implement a comprehensive API security strategy to protect your business from unrestricted access to sensitive business flows.
