
# Section 4: Stakeholder & Conflict Management

## Q1: How do you manage stakeholders and ensure alignment

- Stakeholder Mapping  
  - Work with product, architecture, platform, and operations teams  

- Alignment  
  - Align regularly on priorities through roadmap discussions and reviews  

- Communication  
  - Provide clear updates on progress, risks, and timelines  

- Proactive Engagement  
  - Involve stakeholders early in decisions  

- Outcome  
  - Ensures alignment and avoids surprises  

---

## Q2: How do you handle stakeholder conflicts and difficult situations

- Situation  
  - Conflicts arise due to competing priorities like feature delivery vs platform or compliance changes  

- Approach  
  - Align stakeholders on business goals rather than opinions  
  - Understand each stakeholder’s priorities  

- Decision  
  - Evaluate trade-offs like cost, timeline, and risk  
  - Present options with pros and cons  

- Resolution  
  - Drive toward a balanced decision or phased approach  
  - Escalate if needed with data-backed recommendations  

- Outcome  
  - Ensures objective and business-aligned decisions  

---

## Q3: Tell me about a real conflict you handled

- Situation  
  - Architecture team wanted immediate observability migration, while my team was focused on a compliance-driven model upgrade  

- Challenge  
  - Both initiatives were important with conflicting timelines  

- Action  
  - I explained business priority and proposed a phased approach  

- Result  
  - Completed model upgrade first, followed by observability adoption  

- Learning  
  - Importance of prioritization and stakeholder alignment  

---

## Q4: How do you influence product and architecture decisions

- Understanding  
  - Understand business goals, requirements, and constraints  

- Input  
  - Provide inputs on feasibility, effort, and technical risks  

- Trade-offs  
  - Balance feature delivery with tech debt and platform needs  

- Alignment  
  - Work toward a realistic and achievable roadmap  

- Outcome  
  - Ensures practical and aligned decision-making  

---

## Q5: How do you handle dependencies and escalations

- Identification  
  - Identify cross-team dependencies early during planning  

- Tracking  
  - Track progress and follow up regularly  

- Communication  
  - Provide clear visibility on risks and timelines  

- Escalation  
  - Escalate early with context and solution options  

- Outcome  
  - Prevents delays and ensures smooth execution  

---

## Q6: How do you build trust with stakeholders

- Transparency  
  - Provide honest and consistent updates  

- Reliability  
  - Deliver on commitments consistently  

- Collaboration  
  - Involve stakeholders in key decisions  

- Outcome  
  - Builds long-term trust and strong working relationships
