# Section 4: Stakeholder & Conflict Management

## Q1: How do you manage stakeholders and ensure alignment
- Stakeholder Mapping - Work with product, architecture, platform, and operations teams  
- Alignment - Sync regularly on priorities through roadmap discussions and reviews  
- Communication - Provide clear updates on progress, risks, and timelines  
- Proactive Engagement - Involve stakeholders early in key decisions  
- Outcome - Ensure alignment and avoid surprises  

---

## Q2: How do you handle stakeholder conflicts and difficult situations
- Situation - Conflicts arise due to competing priorities like features vs platform or compliance  
- Alignment - Align stakeholders on business goals rather than opinions  
- Understanding - Capture each stakeholder’s priorities and constraints  
- Decision - Evaluate trade-offs like cost, timeline, and risk and present options  
- Resolution - Drive balanced or phased solution and escalate if needed  
- Outcome - Ensure objective and business-aligned decisions  

---

## Q3: Tell me about a real conflict you handled
- Situation - Observability migration vs compliance-driven model upgrade conflict  
- Challenge - Both initiatives critical with conflicting timelines  
- Action - Explained business priority and proposed phased approach  
- Result - Completed model upgrade first, then observability adoption  
- Learning - Importance of prioritization and stakeholder alignment  

---

## Q4: How do you influence product and architecture decisions
- Understanding - Align with business goals, requirements, and constraints  
- Input - Provide feasibility, effort, and technical risk insights  
- Trade-offs - Balance features with tech debt and platform changes  
- Alignment - Guide toward realistic and achievable roadmap  
- Outcome - Enable practical and informed decisions  

---

## Q5: How do you handle dependencies and escalations
- Identification - Identify cross-team dependencies early during planning  
- Tracking - Monitor progress and follow up regularly  
- Communication - Provide visibility into risks and timelines  
- Escalation - Escalate early with context and solution options  
- Outcome - Prevent delays and ensure smooth execution  

---

## Q6: How do you build trust with stakeholders
- Transparency - Provide honest and consistent updates  
- Reliability - Deliver on commitments consistently  
- Collaboration - Involve stakeholders in key decisions  
- Outcome - Build long-term trust and strong relationships
